- Expected 2026 revenue: +$3.5M

## Finding #2: Seasonal Patterns

### Pattern 1: January Peak (Volume-Driven)
- **Revenue**: $6.88M (highest of year)
- **Driver**: ~5,000 orders (10% above average)
- **Avg Price**: $115 (below average)
- **Implication**: Customers buying in bulk, lower margins

### Action: January "Logistics" Play
- **Need**: +10% operational capacity
- **Investment**: Seasonal staffing, temp warehousing
- **Timeline**: Hire Oct-Dec for Jan spike
- **ROI**: Avoid lost sales due to stockouts

### Pattern 2: March Peak (Value-Driven)
- **Revenue**: $1.58M
- **Driver**: Highest avg price ($131, 10% above average)
- **Volume**: Moderate (~3,500 orders)
- **Implication**: Customers willing to pay premium

### Action: March "Premium" Play
- **Strategy**: Bundle high-margin products
- **Marketing**: Premium positioning
- **Target Lift**: 5-10% increase in order value
- **Expected Revenue**: +$200-400K

### Pattern 3: April Trough (Double-Crash)
- **Revenue**: $5.0M (lowest of year)
- **Driver**: Both volume AND price crash
- **Root Cause**: Post-season slowdown + inventory overstock
- **Loss**: ~$1M monthly opportunity

### Action: April "Spring Recovery" Campaign
- **Tactics**: Flash sales, clearance, new product launches
- **Timeline**: March 15 - April 30
- **Target**: Bring April to $5.5M+ baseline
- **Expected Revenue**: +$300-500K
